KFC HISTORY

1

Established by Colonel Harland Sanders, KFC has come a long way since its early days. Colonel Sanders had a tough task at his hand at breaking through the dominance of hamburgers in the fast food scene in the days of Great Depression. Soon it began to find takers across all ages.

2

Colonel Sanders began selling fried chicken from his roadside restaurant in Corbin, Kentucky during the grim times of the Great Depression.

3

Identifying the potential of his delicious chicken and the change it can bring about in a restaurant franchising concept, the first "Kentucky Fried Chicken" franchise opened in Utah in 1952.
